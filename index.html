About

I'm a game designer who likes systems that misbehave in interesting ways. Most of my favorite work has come from the moment a spreadsheet model breaks — that's usually where the real design problem was hiding. I've worked across combat balancing, live-ops economy tuning, and narrative branching, and I'm equally happy deep in a probability curve or watching a playtester rage-quit a boss fight I clearly need to fix.

Before games, I studied applied math, which mostly comes up now when I'm arguing with an engineer about damage formulas. I write design docs that engineers actually read, and I playtest obsessively — usually starting with paper before a single line of code exists.
